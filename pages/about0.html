Founded in 2022 by Rohil Patel, James Langile, and Ean Dela Cruz.

Hi! We're students at BCIT under the CST Diploma program. We know very well how busy our lives can get sometimes. Taking 7 courses in our first term gave us the idea to create a scheduling app that will help manage our time. We also thought of those who aren't just students, but people that work part-time or full-time. It's important for us to be able to enjoy life, but it's not always so easy to make that time.

The Schedue app is a mobile friendly web application that helps our user find time to make time. We wanted to create a solution for busy students or workers to manage their time, because we know that life can get hectic quickly. The app allows for students and workers to update their calanders with upcomming events that gets converted into their personal to-do list.
